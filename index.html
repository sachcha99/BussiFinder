What is BussiFinder

"BussiFinder" is a web application to process the best solution that makes a successful business.

Location is a key factor for the success of a new business. Therefore, selection of a location is one of the business decisions that have to be made carefully. As a solution for this decision-making process,we have proposed this "BussiFinder" application to analyze the given location and generate an accurate business solution for the user. Using this application, users can find which business is suitable for their land. This proposed application is not only focused on landowners who are going to start a new business but also focused on people who are willing to buy land or rent a building to start a new business. Additionally, the Application helps users to compare different locations before taking a final decision.

Background and Literature Survey

The success of a business is mostly dependent upon the business’s location. There are few researchers who have built several products to make this decision making process easy. And different researchers have used different approaches to get a solution for this issue. However, most of the time those products were narrowed down to one business. Because of that from users’ perspective, they have to decide what business they are going to start, and only after that decision users can choose an application or a product to check whether their decision is right or wrong. This situation will force users to use several applications to get the most suitable business that users can start. In 2009 Joshua K. Ladle and David and Duane Stiller released a research paper regarding retail site selection. As the researchers described their research uses a new, innovative model for retail development. Furthermore, Mengwen Xu, Tianyi Wang, Zhengwei Wu, Jingbo Zhou, Jian Li, and Haishan Wu research team conducted research on Demand-Driven Store Site Selection via Multiple Spatial-temporal Data. In this research for the data and preprocessing propose the research team has used map query data from Baidu Maps contains over one billion queries from 500 million users.

Research Problem

Selecting the best suitable business type for a location is challenging and it should be strategically decided by the business stakeholders. Business location and the way of providing customer services through the business has a tight relationship with business successes. The more favorable the location and the customer service of a business, the higher possibility of the business succeeding. Lack of experience in choosing a business type with considering location factors can lead to financial losses, negative impact on a brand name, unsatisfied customers, and competitors gaining market share. Several factors can affect the success of the business under the place to start the business. Location factors that influence business success vary with different business types .

Research Objectives

Based on the user's input data and an evaluation of the location-related success characteristics discovered, the tool will effectively provide a success percentage to the related business type. We are using an algorithm to analyze the location using factors we are going to consider. The technology will assist in making informed business decisions, and higher levels of customer satisfaction will boost customer loyalty from visitors and hasten the return on investment

Methodology

the data set gathered for this study as well as the elements taken into consideration when choosing an appropriate location for the pertinent business kinds. These evaluation criteria were chosen after considering previous examples and investigating the success of nearby businesses. In this study 1998 hotels, 1988 Restaurants, 1500 Pharmacies, 1651 Groceries from all over the Sri Lanka were obtained from the google maps[18]. We have used the Google Map API, Place API, Geocode API, Distance Matrix API, and Traffic API to analyze the factors that suitable for the relevant business type. The identified factors were used to train several machine learning models to predict the success of the business. We have used the Python Flask API to deploy the trained models and exposed to client applications
